Monthly Prayer Meeting

We gather on the third Saturday of every month at Mount Tabor (2363 Hunter Mill Road) at 6PM for bible study, the divine mercy chaplet, and praise and worship, followed by dinner and fellowship!

Weekly Prayer Call

As a member of St. Mark's Small Communities of Faith, join us every Wednesday from 8 to 9PM EST for bible study and intercessory prayer by calling 605-475-4842 and entering the code 699026. Note it may ask you to announce your name, please ignore this request.

Daily Prayer Call

Join us every evening from 9 to 9:15PM EST for the divine mercy chaplet and intercessory prayer by calling 605-475-4842 and entering the code 699026. Note it may ask you to announce your name, please ignore this request.
